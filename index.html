Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana , children are given their first name as a 'day name'
which corresponds to the day of the week they were born.

The application prompts the user to enter the day,month and year-of-birth to get their Akan names from the Ghanian culture depending on the week they were born.
The application runs in search for the key Id which checks picks the akan names according to gender
